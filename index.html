Who We Are

Founded in 2005, Greengate is an independent financial advisory firm that specializes in complex project, corporate, and hybrid debt financings for energy and infrastructure projects around the globe. We have extensive experience in a range of industry sectors

Over two decades, the firm has built a reputation as one of one of world's premier independent financial advisors.

We advise developers and sponsors, financial institutions, governments, and institutional investors on the financing of infrastructure projects globally, including some of the largest and most complex project financings. To date, Greengate has advised on over $450 billion in infrastructure assets across more than 60 countries.

What We Do

Sponsors

We work with project developers and sponsors to formulate a bankable commercial structure and financing plan, and assist in negotiating and executing the optimal capital structure. We often become involved early in project development and continue to work with the client through to a successful financial closing.

Lenders

We assist financing institutions, including commercial lenders and government financing agencies, in the evaluation and negotiation of large project finance loan transactions. Greengate has a distinguished record of successful engagements involving export credit agencies, multilateral banks and other national and international public sector lenders and guarantors.

Restructuring

Our skills and experience are also applied to restructuring and value recovery of distressed project debt. We have worked with lender syndicates to maximize recovery values for defaulted projects, and with distressed borrowers in default or near-default scenarios.

Government Entities

Greengate works with state-owned companies and other public sector entities to assess the economic and financial viability of developing strategically significant, major infrastructure projects.
